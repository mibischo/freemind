Detailed Description

This is the central method interface of actions that can be undertaken on nodes. Whenever you want to change the mindmap choose one of these actions as they do proper redisplay, inform others about the actions, the actions are all undoable etc.etc.

All these methods do redisplay, because they are offered from the MindMapController for use.

Author
foltin see MindMapController
